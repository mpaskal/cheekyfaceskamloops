Face Paint Durability and Care

How long does the face paint last?
Our face paints are designed to last the entire event and can stay vibrant for several hours if left untouched. However, they can smudge if rubbed or exposed to excessive water.
How can the face paint be cleaned off?
The face paint can be easily removed with mild soap and warm water. A soft washcloth or gentle makeup remover can help with any stubborn areas. No harsh scrubbing is required!
What happens if it rains? Will the face paint wash off immediately?
Our paints are water-based and will smudge if exposed to heavy rain or excessive moisture. For outdoor events, we recommend having a covered or shaded area to ensure everyone stays dry and comfortable.
What can I do to ensure the face paint lasts longer?
Encourage kids to avoid rubbing their faces and stay away from water activities like swimming. For longer-lasting results, we recommend designs on arms or hands, which are less likely to smudge.
